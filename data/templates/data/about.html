-ome: The complete whole of a class of substances for a species or an individual.


What is HTFome?

HTFome is a web application designed to give users access to data on Human Transcription Factors (HTFs), drugs which target HTFs, and allows users to upload GEO DataSets for further analysis.

This webapp was developed as part of the Queen Mary University of London (QMUL) Bioinformatics masters degree program.

Developers of this app were A.M. Andersen, K. Antoniuk, N. Hardie and A. Sutradhar. The source code can be found on GitHub.
